Company Leave Policy

Full-time employees are eligible for 12 casual leaves per calendar year.

Employees are also eligible for 10 sick leaves per calendar year.

Unused casual leaves cannot be carried forward to the next calendar year.

Sick leaves can be carried forward up to a maximum of 20 days.

Employees must apply for planned leave at least 3 working days in advance.

Leave requests longer than 5 consecutive working days require manager approval and HR approval.

During probation, employees are eligible for 1 casual leave per month.

The notice period for permanent employees is 60 days.
