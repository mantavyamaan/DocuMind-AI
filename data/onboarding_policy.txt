Company Onboarding Policy

New employees must submit identity proof, address proof, educational certificates, previous employment documents, PAN card, and bank account details.

The onboarding process must be completed within 5 working days from the joining date.

HR will conduct an orientation session on the first working day.

IT assets such as laptop, email account, VPN access, and ID card will be provided after document verification.

Employees must complete information security training within 10 working days of joining.
