Company Reimbursement Policy

Employees can claim meal reimbursement up to Rs. 500 per working day during approved business travel.

Hotel reimbursement is capped at Rs. 4,000 per night for domestic travel.

Taxi reimbursement is allowed only for official travel and must include valid receipts.

All reimbursement claims must be submitted within 15 days of expense date.

Claims above Rs. 10,000 require finance manager approval.

International travel reimbursement requires prior approval from the department head.
